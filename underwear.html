Best Types of Underwear to Wear During Periods

Choosing the right underwear during your period can significantly improve your comfort and hygiene. Here are our top recommendations:

1. Cotton Underwear

  • Cotton underwear is highly breathable and helps to keep moisture away, reducing the risk of irritation and infections.
  • Soft and gentle on the skin, reducing irritation.
  • While not as absorbent as specialized period panties, cotton can still help manage minor leaks and sweat.
  • Best Brands: Hanes, Fruit of the Loom, Jockey

2. Period Panties

  • Period panties are specially designed to provide extra protection and absorbency, making them a great option for heavy flow days.
  • They have multiple layers and are made from materials that prevent leaks and stains.
  • Made from soft, breathable fabrics, they reduce discomfort and chafing.
  • Best Brands: Thinx, Knix, Modibodi

3. Seamless Underwear

  • Seamless underwear prevents visible panty lines and offers a smooth fit, ensuring comfort and confidence during your period.
  • No seams mean less irritation and chafing, making them comfortable for all-day wear.
  • They stretch and adapt to body movements, maintaining comfort throughout the day.
  • Best Brands: Calvin Klein, Commando, Spanx

4. High-Waisted Underwear

  • High-waisted underwear can provide additional support and coverage, which can be particularly comforting during your period.
  • They offer extra support around the abdomen, which can be soothing if you're experiencing cramps.
  • The higher waist can help prevent leaks from the top or sides.
  • Best Brands: Hanky Panky, Bali, Vanity Fair

5. Boy Shorts

  • Boy shorts offer full coverage, which helps keep pads in place and provides a sense of security.
  • The wider sides reduce the risk of chafing and are often more comfortable for extended wear.
  • They allow for ease of movement and are less likely to bunch up.
  • Best Brands: Calvin Klein, Hanes, Victoria's Secret

6. Full Briefs

  • Full briefs provide maximum coverage, reducing the risk of leaks.
  • They offer good support for pads, making them less likely to shift.
  • Generally made from soft, breathable fabrics, they are comfortable to wear.
  • Best Brands: Sloggi, Chantelle, Warner's
